## Algorithmic Complexity

Example complexity expressions:

Time: $O(n \log n)$  
Space: $O(n)$

A recurrence:

$$
T(n) = T(n/2) + O(n)
$$

Therefore:

$$
T(n) = O(n \log n)
$$
